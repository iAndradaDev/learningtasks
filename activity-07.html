MY FAVORITE DISH

Pork Sinigang

INGREDIENTS

  • Pork belly, ribs, or shoulder
  • Tamarind mix
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Taro (gabi)
  • Radish (labanos)
  • String beans (sitaw)
  • Eggplant (talong)
  • Okra
  • Water spinach (kangkong)
  • Long green chilies (siling haba)
  • Water (or rice washing)
  • Fish sauce (patis)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

STEPS

  1. Boil and tenderize Pork belly, ribs, or shoulder
  2. Thicken with taro
  3. Sour the broth
  4. Layer the vegetables
  5. Finish with greens
